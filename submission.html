Submit a Manuscript

Manuscripts are submitted through an online form. Before submitting, please read the Author Guidelines and Publication Policies.

Submit Manuscript

The form requires sign-in with a Google account (needed for file upload). If you cannot use the form, email your submission files to the editorial office: sunwchung@jnu.ac.kr.

Required Files

Prepare the following files before opening the submission form:

FileNotes
Manuscript file .docx, double-spaced, numbered pages and lines, no author names (for review) — download template
Title page Separate file with title, all authors, affiliations, and corresponding author — download template
AI use statement Brief description of any AI tools used, or a statement that none were used
Figure files If applicable: PNG, JPEG, or TIFF, one file per figure

Submission Checklist

  • ☐ The first author is an undergraduate student
  • ☐ The manuscript follows the Author Guidelines
  • ☐ The abstract is 250 words or fewer, with 4–6 keywords
  • ☐ References are complete and accurate
  • ☐ Figures and tables are numbered and captioned
  • ☐ The AI use statement is included
  • ☐ All co-authors have approved the submission
  • ☐ The work is original and not under review elsewhere

What Happens Next

  1. The editorial office confirms that the submission is complete and within scope.
  2. The manuscript is assigned to faculty reviewers.
  3. Authors receive a decision: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, or Reject.
  4. Accepted manuscripts are copyedited, formatted, and published in the current volume.

Questions about a submission may be directed to the editorial office — see the Contact page.
