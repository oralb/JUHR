Method Note

A Low-Cost, Smartphone Image-Based Method for Measuring Leaf Area

Seo J.1 and Moon H.1

1 Department of Horticulture, Chonnam National University, Gwangju, Republic of Korea

Received: pending · Accepted: pending · Published: 2026
JUHR 1(1): 26–29 · DOI: pending

Abstract

[Sample placeholder abstract.] Leaf area is a fundamental measurement in horticultural research, but commercial leaf area meters are expensive and often unavailable to student researchers. This method note describes a workflow for measuring leaf area using a smartphone camera, a reference scale card, and free image analysis software. Validation against a commercial leaf area meter across three crop species showed close agreement. The full protocol, including imaging guidelines and common error sources, is provided so that other undergraduate researchers can apply the method directly.

Keywords: leaf area, image analysis, low-cost methods, smartphone, measurement
