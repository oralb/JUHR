Awards

JUHR recognizes outstanding undergraduate scholarship each publication year. Award winners are selected by the editorial board from articles published in the corresponding volume.

Best Paper Award

Awarded to the most outstanding article of the volume across all article types, based on the importance of the question, the quality of the reasoning and evidence, and the clarity of the writing.

Best Review Article Award

Awarded to the strongest review article or systematic review, recognizing thorough literature synthesis, critical evaluation, and scholarly insight.

Best Hypothesis Paper Award

Awarded to the most original and logically developed hypothesis paper, recognizing creative scientific thinking grounded in published evidence.

Outstanding Undergraduate Research Award

Awarded to an undergraduate first author whose research article demonstrates exceptional initiative, rigor, and contribution to horticultural science.

Award Recipients

The first awards will be announced after the completion of Volume 1 (2026).

Eligibility

All articles published in JUHR are automatically considered for awards in their publication year. No separate application is required.
